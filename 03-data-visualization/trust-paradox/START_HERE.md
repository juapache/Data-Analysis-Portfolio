## üéØ The Core Idea (30 seconds)

**The Trust Paradox** shows that:

1. **Heavy security alone doesn't create sustainable peace**  
   ‚ùå Security suppresses violence (good)  
   ‚ùå But erodes trust if excessive (bad for sustainability)

2. **Trust alone isn't enough**  
   ‚úÖ Trust enables cooperation (good)  
   ‚úÖ But without enforcement, vulnerable to predators (risky)

3. **Institutions bridge the gap**  
   ‚úÖ Fair institutions earn trust  
   ‚úÖ Legitimate institutions can enforce rules without oppression

**Solution**: Balance all three dimensions for sustainable peace.

---

## üìä What The Model Measures

Three dimensions (0‚Äì100 scale):
- **Trust (T)**: Do people trust each other & the government?
- **Security (S)**: How much enforcement, surveillance, protection?
- **Institutions (I)**: How fair, inclusive, capable?

Three outputs (0‚Äì100 scale):
- **Negative Peace**: Absence of violence (mainly security + institutions)
- **Positive Peace**: Presence of justice & wellbeing (mainly trust + institutions)
- **Sustainability**: Resilience to shocks (balance across all three)
